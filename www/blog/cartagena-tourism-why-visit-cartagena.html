Cartagena Tourism | Why Visit Cartagena de Indias?

Travel to Cartagena and visit the city’s top wonders!

Cartagena plays a significant role in Colombia’s economic and industrial breakthrough. Aside from being a major part of the country, it has been known worldwide due to its thriving tourism. It may not be as competitive as Bogota, but this vibrant city takes pride with its amazing beaches and tempting recreational activities.

To experience the beauty of Cartagena tourism, one must take into account some travel-worthy spots of the city. Below are some:

  • The City

    • “Old is gold” - Cartagena’s city itself is a major attraction. Dubbed as a UNESCO World Heritage Site, the old town is one of the well-preserved primitive architectures in the American region. The Walled City was erected to protect its people from pirates and marauders. The walls were also built to defend the city, eventually becoming a historic site in the country. When you travel to Cartagena and walk around the town, you will be amazed to witness majestic buildings designed under colonial times. If you are fond of visiting historical places, Cartagena suits best for you!

  • Convento de la Popa

    • If you want to witness a panoramic view of Cartagena, Convento de la Popa is the perfect choice for you! The convent stands on the top of a hill. It was originally a small chapel but was later fortified and strengthened. The convent also showcases the perfect view of the sunset in the city. Due to its romantic ambiance during sunsets, this has become a perfect haven for foreigners who date Cartagena women.

Discover the beauty of the tourism in Cartagena.
  • Rosario Islands

    • Your trip to Cartagena won’t be complete if you miss out on the Rosario Islands. These islands showcase pristine waters and white sand beaches. You will be amazed by how the people preserved their aquamarine life. Some of the most famous beaches include Playa Blanca, Isla Grande, Isla Cholon, and Bendita Beach. You can do Kayaking, Snorkeling, and swim to witness the beauty under the waters. These are just some of the leisurely activities and things to do in Cartagena.

  • El Totumo

    • El Totumo is the smallest volcano in Colombia. It measures only 49 ft, and has a staircase should you want to see its crater. This mud volcano is known for its healing mud bath. Aside from its alleged therapeutic capacity, you will be more relaxed after receiving massages from the attendants. A nearby lagoon is also accessible close to the volcano to ward off the mud.

  • Entertainment

    • Nightlife in Cartagena is an exciting moment you need to experience. Various performances are performed, sometimes in the streets of the city or near the Clock Tower, a famous landmark in the vicinity of Cartagena. Here, you will be amazed by how Cartagena individuals celebrate and show their prosperity. Aside from that, you will come to realize that the liveliest people live in Cartagena, Colombia.

Mentioned above are the signs of the thriving tourism in Cartagena. The city features iconic strongholds and historic structures that won’t only bless your eyes, but also have you ponder that the success of a city or a country does not merely rely on its governance, but also on its constituents and the beauty surrounding it.
