What Types of Gifts Colombian Women LOVE to Receive

Giving Colombian women surprise gifts just makes their day! | Photo by Pablo Merchan Montes on Unsplash

Choosing a gift can be quite difficult. You can have the widest variety of items to choose from, and still have no idea what to get. At some point in our life, you may have eventually given up and resorted to picking the first random item off the shelf.

But there is actually a reason behind why we struggle to choose a gift — it’s because we consciously think about how the person receiving it would feel and react.

Gift-giving is a way to show your appreciation. Your present does not need to be expensive to delight the recipient; it’s about thanking and expressing gratitude. As the famous saying goes, it’s the thought that counts. While that holds true, there are some instances that gifts that are of no use get tossed aside. And it does happen to everyone, whether we like it or not.

Gifts are much more than just what’s in the box. These items hold certain meanings to them.

Let’s say you plan to give your Columbian girlfriend a ring. To others, a ring is just an accessory. But that crafted piece of precious metal (encrusted with jewels) also symbolizes devotion, fidelity and eternity. It’s a simple piece of accessory, yet it holds so much weight in a relationship.

Choosing Gifts for Colombian Women

People are likely to enjoy receiving gifts in general. While the gesture is appreciated, this feeling only lasts for a fleeting moment until they eventually forget about it.

If you are dating a Colombian woman, you can’t settle for mediocre gifts. With her beauty and personality, she is sure to have admirers and suitors, and it’ll be a tough competition to win her heart. If you want to be able to leave an impact, might as well give her a meaningful and memorable present.

Thinking about what are the best gifts for Colombian women? Start with the things that she needs. Better yet, choose an item that will solve her day to day problems to make her life easier. I mean, novelty items are good and all, but they don’t hold much to them except for their sentimental value.

Practical items, on the other hand, can be of great use to her on a regular basis. Items like a file case, a lamp, a waterproof phone case or even a breakfast table — the possibilities are endless!

For a better understanding of what to get your Columbian girlfriend, here are is a list of things you should consider when choosing a gift:

  • Lifestyle

It’s easy to tailor your gift choices when you consider her lifestyle. The main goal here is to identify what her unmet needs are and quench that thirst. It’s the little things that help you choose the perfect gift.

If she is a busy body, get her something that can help her manage her schedule. A calendar, maybe, so she can be up to date with important occasions. A notebook can also be handy when she needs to jot down important information.

These items may seem ordinary, but would mean a lot to her. It will serve as a constant reminder of how you care about her. You may never know, you might have given her what she needed all along.

  • Hobbies and Interests

Hobbies take us out of our ordinary life to do something we love. Think about what she enjoys doing on her own time and how you can improve that experience.

Say for example, your girlfriend is a plant enthusiast. However, her cat keeps them knocking the small potted plants over. To solve this problem, you get her a hanging planter or a wall mount. Not only does it save her the hassle of cleaning up after the mess, but it also keeps her precious plants in good condition.

You can easily narrow down your choices if you are in tune with her taste in music, books or movies. A limited edition book of her favorite novelist or a deluxe album of her favorite artist will leave her pleasantly surprised of how attentive you are to her interests.

  • Occasion

There are many reasons why you should give a gift. The question is, do you know how to choose them according to the occasion?

Of course, special occasions such as anniversaries need more prior planning. This isn’t your run of the mill no occasion gift giving — this is your anniversary! You ALWAYS have to consider the message that comes across the gift. Otherwise she might misunderstand, and we really want to avoid doing that.

But for simple occasions like housewarming parties, it’s okay to keep it lowkey. If you are planning on giving her a Traditional Colombian Housewarming Gift, you can try giving her home decor. The pantry staples you can find in Home decor Stores would be of great use to her if she is fond of cooking.

To tell you frankly, you don’t really need an occasion to give gifts. Sometimes you give them just because. Think about it — it’s really romantic when someone takes the time of their day to surprise us, no matter how simple it is.

The Perfect Gift Ideas

Now comes the fun part - Gift ideas! There are a plethora of gifts you can give your Columbian girlfriend. After carefully considering the factors above, here are some gift ideas your Columbian girlfriend will love:

  • Clothes - a classic yet timeless gift. What we wear says a lot about ourselves. If you manage to get her an article of clothing that is to her taste, she’ll come to understand how you recognize her taste in fashion.

  • Accessories - if you are celebrating a special occasion, it’s best to buy her an accessory. Go for the items that go well with women during the day. You don’t necessarily have to choose the pricey ones, even the simplest watch from you is priceless to her eyes.

  • Stationery - these items may seem ordinary but they’re pretty well-loved. Not only is it practical, but it’s something you can see her using regularly.

  • Food - as odd as it sounds, food actually makes a pretty good gift. Everybody loves the taste of good food. From chocolates to full course meals, there is no limit to what food you can choose! At the same time, you should be careful of any allergies she might have.

These items may look basic, but these Colombian Women will surely appreciate it. Take your time (but not too long!) to choose the perfect gift, and always remember — it’s the thought that counts.
