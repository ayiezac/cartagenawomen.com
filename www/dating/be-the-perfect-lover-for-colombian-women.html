Understanding Colombian Women

Part of playing the game is knowing what the rules are. Remember, you aren’t just dating a woman because she is beautiful, you are dating the totality of who she is. The typical Colombian woman is known to be passionate, caring, thoughtful, and emotional.

In other words, these ladies wear their hearts on their sleeves and are unabashed about expressing their feelings.

But there are in fact men who see this as the makeup for a colorful and exciting relationship. If you intend to date a Colombian woman, you might be the latter.

Colombian women are known to be flaky, spontaneous, and emotional. This isn’t something you can change, but rather accept and adapt to. It simply means that you need to be flexible and open to adventure. This means that your life is going to get all the more colorful.

Dating Colombian Women

Now that you know the kind of woman you intend to date, the key is being attractive enough to get her attention. Your job is to break down these barriers and get them interested in the only other person that matters - you.

The first step is being an absolute gentleman. The old-fashioned way of romance is still something that appeals to Latin American people in general. The closer you are to the quintessential casanova, the better. Don’t let this discourage you, it’s simply about sticking to the basics.
