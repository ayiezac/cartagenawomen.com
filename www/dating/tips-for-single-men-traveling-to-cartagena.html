The Art of Attracting Cartagena Women

Besides the obvious answers such as: be a gentleman, act respectful, look your best, and be charming, take note of these useful and effective tips when dating in Cartagena:

But if you want to be extra, take a dancing class. There are tons of places in the city that offer the course, such as these two places below:

  • Donde Fidel Salsa Bar | Portal de los Dulces, Carrera 4, Cartagena, Colombia

  • Bazurto Social Club | Carrera 9 #30-42, Cartagena, Colombia

3. Be bold and confident - Don’t be timid. Shyness won’t get you anywhere - at least with women in Colombia. They prefer men who can take charge and make them feel pursued. When approaching Colombian women in public spaces, you need to exude confidence and show assertiveness.

6. Lastly, use common sense and trust your instincts. Wherever you are in the world, be it Cartagena or any other foreign city, it’s always best to exercise caution.
